Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Hotwire Hot Rate deal?
Hot Rate deals are special deep-discount offers where the exact brand name of the hotel or car rental company is kept secret until the booking is paid and confirmed. This allows luxury brands to sell extra inventory without diluting their standard pricing.
Can I cancel or change a Hot Rate booking?
To offer such massive discounts (up to 60% off standard rates), Hot Rate hotel bookings and car rentals are non-refundable, non-changeable, non-transferable, and non-cancelable. Please double-check your check-in dates and location before paying.
When will my credit card be charged?
Your credit card will be charged immediately for the full amount of the booking upon clicking "Complete Secure Booking". This ensures your reservation is locked in at the secret discounted rate.
What identification is required at check-in?
You must present a government-issued photo ID (driver's license or passport) and a physical credit card in the primary traveler's name. The hotel/car provider requires this for security deposits and incidental charges.
